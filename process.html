    Multimedia Collage


    This collage was made to honor my grandmother, Marilyn Nugent. I started with a black-and-white photograph of my grandmother on her wedding day when she was 20 years old. I then modified the RGB channels of the photograph in Adobe Photoshop to create the illusion of color. I then printed out the result at a 12x18 size and attached it to a thin canvas. Finally, I covered the canvas with multicolored recycled objects and junk jewelry, matching the color of the objects to that of the photograph.
